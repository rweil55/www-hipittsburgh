Trails in Western Pennsylvania

  • Baker Trail -- A hiking trail from Freeport to the Allegheny National Forest - built and maintained by Pittsburgh AYH.
  • Rachel Carson Trail -- A hiking trail in the northern part of Allegheny County built and maintained by Pittsburgh AYH.
  • Butler Freeport Community Trail  -- A rail-trail in Butler County
  • The Montour Trail -- An excellent rail-trail in Allegheny and Washington Counties 
  • The Armstrong Trail -- A rail-trail following the east shore of the Allegheny River from Schenley (near the start of the Baker Trail) to East Brady. A project of the Allegheny Valley Trust..
  • The Appalachian Trail
  • The North Country Trail
    • The North Country Trail in Pennsylvania
  • Rail-Trails -- More information on rail-trails from the authors of Free Wheeling Easy.
  • Allegheny Trail Alliance -- The story of the member trails from Pittsburgh to Cumberland.
  • The Steel Heritage Trail rail-trail runs along the Monongahela River from Sandcastle Park, through Homestead, past Kennywood Park, across the River to McKeesport, and south through Glassport and Clairton.
  • The Three Rivers Heritage Trail -- a rail-trail running for 15 miles along the Pittsburgh rivers between Washington's Landing and Sandcastle Park.
