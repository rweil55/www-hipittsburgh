Whitewater Kayaking

All boaters bring: Non-cotton paddling clothes, tie-on tennis shoes, sunscreen, nylon wind/rain breaker, straps for glasses & sunglasses, tie-on "waterproof" lunch and plenty of drinking water for in boat, change of clothes, cash  for car-pool gas and dinner on way home.

Check the latest newsletter for the current trip schedule and leader. Call the leader listed for information about a trip or to sign up for the trip.  Please call before 10 o'clock at night.
