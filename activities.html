Outdoor Activities Program

Pittsburgh AYH sponsors many outdoor activities every month.  AYH activities were primarily low-impact recreational trips at the beginner and intermediate level. We have our own equipment for many of the activities and frequently have schools for beginners. Both members and non-members were welcome. Just give the volunteer trip leader a call at the listed telephone number and ask for details on the trip.
