World Travel 101:

Free Budget Travel Workshops

Fall/Winter 1998-1999

Safe, Inexpensive World Travel Is Possible!

Let Hostelling International show you how: We'll provide information on trip planning, necessary documents, health precautions, working along the way, all about your money, and more. Presented by the experienced staff and volunteers of the Pittsburgh International Hostel.

The Workshops were FREE and were offered on the second Wednesday of each month from 7 to 9 pm
