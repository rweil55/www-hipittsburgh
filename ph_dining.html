The Dining Area at the former Pittsburgh International Hostel


 This was the dining area at the former Pittsburgh International Hostel on Warrington . The kitchen was through the door on the right and the common area and elevator were straight ahead through the other door. The dining area and kitchen were on the fourth floor of the hostel. The windows behind you overlook Warrington Avenue. The photo turned out pretty gray -- the table tops were a nice dark green and the walls were beige. Looks great in person; come check it out!
