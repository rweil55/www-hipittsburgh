Bicycle Tours

(Proceeds benefit the Pittsburgh Hostel Fund)
    The Bicycle Tours were one day, mass rides with anywhere from 500 to 2,000 riders, depending on the ride and the weather. They offer mapped and marked routes of various lengths from 20 to 100 miles along with snacks and a lunch. Proceeds benefit the Pittsburgh hostel.  This years rides were:
    • The Great Ride -- July, 2001 Run by Pittsburgh Citiparks with major support from Pittsburgh Council.  Check out the Citiparks Website
    • The Mon Valley Century - August 19, 2001 Sponsored and run by Pittsburgh Council with assistance from the Monesson Amateur Radio Club.
    • SABRE 2001 - Cancelled for year 2001.
    • Add your name to our mailing list for 2001 year rides and beyond.
