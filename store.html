The Pittsburgh Travel Center (closed)

The former Hostelling International - Pittsburgh Travel Center had what you need for budget travel!
  • Hostelling International membership (hostel pass)
  • Free Traveler's Resource Guide for international travel
  • Free hostelling North America Guides and maps
  • Eurail, BritRail, and other European rail passes 
  • International Student/Teacher Identity Card ID cards
  • Let's Go and Lonely Planet travel guides at member discounts
  • Local canoeing, hiking, and cycling guides
  • AYH "I'd Rather Be " and vintage commemorative "Ride" T-Shirts for $2

We were located above the Southside at 830 East Warrington Avenue at the corner with Arlington, in Pittsburgh's Allentown neighborhood. There is map that shows the location, along with textual directions.  Southside is on the south shore of the Monongahela River across from downtown Pittsburgh.
