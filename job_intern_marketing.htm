Hostelling International- American Youth Hostels (HI-AYH) is a National Organization affiliated with the International Youth Hostel Federation (IYHF). Our mission is "To help all, especially the young, gain a greater understanding of the world and its people through hostelling." The Pittsburgh Council of HI-AYH was the local connection to the worldwide chain of over 4500 hostels. The Pittsburgh Council had been in existence since the 1950’s, however it was not until 1997 that a youth hostel was constructed in Pittsburgh. The board of directors was interested in broadening it’s marketing efforts to increase overnights at the hostel and promoting our mission through programming.
